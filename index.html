Privacy Policy

LarsBot respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Information Collected

When you log in using your Epic Games account, LarsBot collects and stores your access tokens securely to provide seamless interaction with Epic Games services via Discord.

Use of Information

Your information is used solely to authenticate your Epic Games account and facilitate the bot's features. We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

Data Security

Access tokens and authentication data are stored securely with encryption and are never exposed publicly.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the bot owner via Discord.
