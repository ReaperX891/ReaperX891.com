About the Bot

This Discord bot is designed to integrate with the Epic Games platform, allowing users to securely log in using their Epic Games account and access Fortnite: Save the World features. Once authenticated, users can view their in-game profile, daily rewards, and other account-related information directly within Discord. The bot ensures a smooth and user-friendly login process by guiding users to obtain an Epic Games authorization code, which is then used to generate access tokens securely. All user data is handled with care and in compliance with our privacy policy.
